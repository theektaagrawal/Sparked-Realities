AR filters and effects bring a spark of magic to everyday moments, transforming them into fun and unforgettable experiences.

As we celebrate this incredible journey of creativity, collaboration, and innovation, join me in reliving the magic and exploring the impact of these engaging, dynamic experiences.

Why Meta Spark AR ?


  • Intuitive Visual Scripting

    Combined an intuitive node-based interface with beginner-friendly tutorials and templates, enabling creators of all skill levels to design interactive AR filters and effects with ease.

  • Rich Library & Creator Community

    Offered a variety of built-in 3D models, textures, and effects, along with access to a thriving community where creators can seek help, share ideas, and learn from others.

  • Wide Audience Reach

    Included real-time simulation tools to preview AR effects and enabled easy publishing to Instagram and Facebook, ensuring creators can reach a vast audience with maximum impact.
